Strategy: Three Streams, Sequenced

Events remain the flagship

Event 1 sold 94/100 tickets in the debut — the format works. Two more in 2026, spaced roughly two months apart. Rolex-tier prize remains the anchor because it's the proven draw. Save prize variation (Omega, Panerai) for 2027 once we've built a three-event track record.

Native raffle launches after Event 2, not before

Originally planned for June via Raffall — pivoted to native Shopify implementation to keep full margin, full data capture, and full brand control. This slips the raffle launch to August, which is actually better: Event 2 creates the warm audience (100 attendees, press, content, social proof) that the raffle launches off, rather than cold Meta traffic. Event drives raffle drives merch drives Event 3. Flywheel.

Merch drops with events, functions as entry multiplier

Every merch purchase auto-enters the raffle (with free-entry path required for legal compliance). Polos and watch case weighted heavier on entries-per-dollar than tees — rewards higher-intent buyers. Merch doubles as revenue engine and raffle marketing.

Watch Club deferred to Q4/2027

Subscription watch club is validated (Watch Gang proves the mass-market model; Verde's play is premium tier). But don't launch a third stream while two are still unproven. Revisit November after Event 3 revenue lands.

Size distribution mandate. Push the supplier for uneven MOQ per size. Target mix per apparel SKU: S 10% / M 25% / L 35% / XL 20% / XXL 10%. M–L–XL = 80% of sales. Equal-size orders kill first-drop economics.

The VT Watch Case is the strategic SKU

At $7 landed with 100 MOQ, this is the highest-margin item in the drop. Recommended retail: $195 (not $150 — leaves money and positioning on the table). Inventory split:

  • 70 units — retail at $195 on verdeettemps.com
  • 24 units — Event 2 gift bag inclusion for Amen Corner + Green Jacket tiers (amplifies perceived ticket value at $7 cost)
  • 6 units — ambassador kits + press gifting for watch-world influencer seeding
Non-negotiable: physical sample before PO. $7 price point is a razor's edge for luxury positioning. Watch case must feel like it belongs next to a Rolex. If sample fails QA, pull from v1 and resource from Spain/Portugal/Italy at $35–50 landed, relaunch Q4.
